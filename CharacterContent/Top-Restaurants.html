Top Restaurants Johannesburg

Homestead

Homestead restaurant is located in Anderson, Johannesburg, and serves traditional South African made food using some of the finest local ingredients. I love this restaurant because you get to build your meal from scratch and choose your sides. You can experience traditional African food in a modern and stylish environment

Moyo Zoo Lake Restaurant

This has to be one of the most relaxing restaurants to eat at in Johannesburg. You have options for various settings but one of my favourites is the upstairs balcony tables looking over the lake while sitting under beautiful trees. This restaurant also provides generous portions of food, you are sure to leave the restaurant completely satisfied.

Marble Restaurant

Fine dining in Rosebank. This restaurant provides an aesthetic environment inside and out. A perfect place for business meetings, birthday dinners, and even a romantic setting for two. Although pricey the food, service, and experience is worth every scent.

Kalahari Thyme Restaurant

A gem, hidden in the Maboneng precinct area. This restaurant is very private and quiet. The food is delicious and the portions are quite generous. The restaurant provides an African experience in the modern world. All I can say is get there as soon as possible, and enjoy!
