Character Rationale

Posted On The Wednesday

Andile Thomas Nkosi is a 25-year old, African male. He is a registered clinical psychologist with a Masters in Psychology. He is fuelled by his passion for understanding the nuances of mental health, especially in the digital age. He considers himself a ‘forever student,’ eager to both build on his academic foundations in psychology and sociology and stays in tune with the latest digital technologies through continued coursework and professional development.

Andile was born in Kimberley, Northern Cape, and is currently residing in Marshalltown, Johannesburg in a 2 bedroom apartment. With average height and build, brown eyes, and dark brown hair, Andile is empathetic and smart as well as slightly hypersensitive. He loves fashion and food and considers himself the ultimate gentleman. His most distinguishing feature is that he has a great smile. His favourite dress code is smart casual, paired with his favourite watch. He has a demanding presence and walks with confidence and power

What provoked the idea of Andile as a character was during my research of internet cultures I had discovered an increase in the number and importance of influencers in today's cyberculture. The fact that anyone with a strong opinion about various brands and products could influence a large group of people just through the products they use and purchased sparked interest in me to create a character who ultimately becomes an influencer to better understand what sparked this trend and why it is of so much importance

Therefore, through Andile being a food blogger have the opportunity to reflect on how much the web has given ordinary people means to create and share their content with people from all over the world. Furthermore, how this has given rise to the cyberculture of influencers,
